Key Insights from COVID-19 Data Analysis:

1. Countries with smaller populations showed higher infection percentages.
2. Death rates varied significantly depending on healthcare infrastructure.
3. Vaccination rollout was uneven across continents.
4. Rolling vaccination data helped track progress over time effectively.
5. Some countries showed rapid vaccination growth after initial delays.

Conclusion:
Vaccination trends and infection rates highlight the importance of timely healthcare response and population density factors.
